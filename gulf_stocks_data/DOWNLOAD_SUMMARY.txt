======================================================================
DATA FORMAT
======================================================================

Each CSV file contains:
  - Date (index): Trading date
  - Open: Opening price
  - High: Highest price
  - Low: Lowest price
  - Close: Closing price
  - Adj Close: Adjusted closing price
  - Volume: Trading volume

Data is sorted from earliest to latest date.
